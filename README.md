AutoX is a web-based platform designed to streamline vehicle service booking and construction material procurement in Sri Lanka. It connects users with reliable vehicle service providers and material suppliers, offering a centralized solution for logistics and transportation needs. The platform features seamless search and filtering for vehicles and materials, role-based dashboards for customers, vehicle owners, and suppliers, and a feedback system to ensure transparency and trust.
